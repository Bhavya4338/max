2014 - F3 Career

Max Verstappen dominated European Formula 3, winning 10 races and catching the attention of F1 teams.

|

2015 - F1 Debut

Became the youngest driver in F1 history at 17, driving for Toro Rosso (now AlphaTauri).

|

2016 - First Win

Mid-season switch to Red Bull Racing. Won his first race in Spain, becoming the youngest race winner in F1.

|

2018 - Becomes Lead Driver

After Ricciardo’s departure, Max became Red Bull’s primary driver, leading the team’s development.

2021 - First Title

Won his first F1 World Championship in a dramatic last-lap battle against Lewis Hamilton in Abu Dhabi.

|

2022 - Second Title

Dominated the season with 15 wins, setting a new record for the most wins in a single season

|

2023 - Third Title

Continued his dominance, securing a third consecutive title with Red Bull’s unmatched performance.

|

2024 - Current Season Performance

Leading the championship battle, continuing to break records and solidify his legacy in F1.
